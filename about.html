Built for every student,
by students.

Must Material was created to solve a single problem: students spending more time hunting for study materials than actually studying. We built a centralized, free, peer-verified hub that covers everything from 6th grade to final-year B.Tech.

All content is reviewed by educators and community moderators. Uploaders earn recognition badges. Future features include interactive campus maps, study roadmaps, and community forums.



Why Share your PDF's?

Sharing your notes, assignments, and PDFs helps everyone learn better:

  • Help others – Your notes might save someone before exams
  • Learn better – Teaching/sharing improves your own understanding
  • Build community – Students support each other
